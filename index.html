🔴 Bist du doof™

Our AI asks the questions nobody wants to ask.

Who actually owns this?

How it works

  1. Find the soft spots. Assumptions, missing owners, “we’ll figure it out later.”
  2. Ask the rude question. The one nobody wants to ask in the meeting.
  3. Turn it into action. Add an owner, a metric, a decision, or a risk response.

Very funny examples

  • “Great idea. Who is going to do it? No, seriously.”
  • “Is this a plan, or a hope with a calendar invite?”
  • “If this slips by 2 weeks, does anything break… or does nobody care?”
  • “Which stakeholder will pretend to agree, then quietly block it?”
  • “If we had to cut 30% of scope tomorrow, what survives?”
Bist du doof™ is internal humor. The function is serious: make weak thinking visible before it becomes expensive.
